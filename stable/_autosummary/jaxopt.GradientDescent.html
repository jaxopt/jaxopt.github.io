prox(hyperparams=None, scaling=1.0)

Proximal operator for \(g(x) = 0\), i.e., the identity function.

Since \(g(x) = 0\), the output is:

\[\underset{y}{\text{argmin}} ~ \frac{1}{2} ||x - y||_2^2 = x\]
